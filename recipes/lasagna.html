Lasagna

Description

Everyone loves a good lasagna, right?
It's a great way to feed a crowd and a perfect dish to bring to a potluck.
It freezes well. It reheats well. Leftovers will keep you happy for days

Ingredients

  • meat
  • onion and garlic
  • tomatoes
  • sugar
  • lasagna noodles
  • spices
  • egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. cook the noodles
  3. layer the lasagna
  4. cover with foil and bake
